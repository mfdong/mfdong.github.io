Hi. This is not my portfolio site. My portfolio is here. This is my space for tinkering with the web and coding for fun.

Coding is something I do at a corporate job, but it's been hard to remember why I started doing it in the first place - after all, it's now my primary source of income. By using it as a form of self-expression, I'm trying to remind myself that I have passions, interests, and desires for this skill outside of money and survival: I like to make art. I like to entertain. I like to tell stories. I like to document my life. I like all of these things, and code is something I want to use to amplify, enhance, and combine these ideas.

With all that being said, welcome. Enjoy your stay.
